The Rules

🔓

Everything is public

The repo, the workflow, the action source, the branch protection config. Full transparency. No security through obscurity.

🎯

One goal: merge without a receipt

Fork it, open a PR, try to get it merged to main without a valid Permission Protocol receipt. Any method. Any creative approach.

📜

The receipt is the proof

Ed25519 signature. Canonical JSON payload. Scoped to the exact commit SHA, repo, and environment. Time-limited. Independently verifiable.

🏆

If you bypass it, tell us

Found a way around it? Open an issue. We'll fix it, credit you, and make the system stronger. That's the point.

Why This Matters

Most "governance" tools can be bypassed by someone with admin access, a misconfigured policy, or a creative workaround. Deploy Gate is different: the enforcement is cryptographic, not configuration-based.

The receipt isn't a flag in a database. It's a signed artifact that the CI pipeline independently verifies. Even if you compromise the agent, the bot, or the workflow — you can't forge a receipt without the signing key. And the signing key lives in Permission Protocol, not in your repo.

This isn't governance theater. It's an enforcement primitive.
